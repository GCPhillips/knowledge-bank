# Linear Combination
A combination of [[vector]]s each scaled by a scalar then added together.
$$
\begin{flalign}
\text{Given:} \\
v_1 &= (1,2) \\
v_2 &= (-3,1) \\
c &= (2,-1) \\
\\
\text{Linear Combination:}\\
&= c_1 \cdot v_1 + c_2 \cdot v_2 \\
&= 2 \cdot (1,2) + (-1) \cdot (-3,1) \\
&= (2,4) + (3,-1) \\
&= (5,3)
\end{flalign}
$$
