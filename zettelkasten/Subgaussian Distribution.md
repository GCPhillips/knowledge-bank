# Subgaussian Distribution

A distribution where the tail ends decay at least as fast as a [[Gaussian/Normal Distribution]].

##### 1-Subgaussian Bound:
$$
P\left(\sum_{i=1}^n x_i \gt \sqrt{2n \log(\frac{1}{\delta})}\right) \leq \delta
$$
